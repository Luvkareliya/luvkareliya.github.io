20 Innovative Products from Kickstarter

Xtronaut 2.0 - Xtronaut 2.0 is an interactive board game of rocket science designed by Dante Lauretta and Michael Lyon. Their objective is to create enthusiasm and unleash creativity in teenagers and young souls. The game is targeting kids and teenagers sections but it is open for all age groups. One resolution brought by Xtronaut 2.0 is they took over unproductive Board games and video games with something which includes real imagination and requires creative and logical thinking.

digitAize - DigitAize is a powerful tool to track, monitor and enhance the performance of acoustic musical instruments like viola, violin and cello. digitAize is equipped with a highly functioning gyroscope which gives a track of instrument’s motion. It displays the note and the scale of the sound, it’s lightweight and can be mounted on any acoustic instrument. digitAize is ideal for people who’ve just started playing an instrument.

GREEN DISC - green disc is a tool targeting the bicycle market segment. It provides a maintenance service to bicycle chains and can be recycled. Green disc eliminates the factors of getting your hands dirty, spilling oil over your clothes and bicycle and much more. Green disc can be reused as it has a section of oil refiling. It gives the advantage of quick and easy refilling whenever you need it.

FireAnt - FireAnt is an accessory for a swiss knife. It’s main purpose is to make sparks using a knife and a rod which is inserted in the corkscrew. It is very convenient and reusable. It targets the market of wilderness explorers and it solves the problem of finding fire stones to light a fire.

BagClamp - BagClamp is a simple yet powerful tool designed by slant 3D production. It uses clamp method to clip grocery bags and a thread based cap to get access to the contents of the bag.

Bucqle - Bucqle is a grand replacement for belts and things we use to keep our pants tight. It uses a clamp method to secure the tightness of the pants. It is small in size making it barely noticeable unlike the regular belts. It is made from sustainable materials which do not include harining animals, while some belts are purely made of leather. Bucqle is a very unique product as it tackles many problems at once while being convenient to carry and use.

IsoSpine - Isospine is a health based product which targets the spine. It has an ergonomic design which helps to relieve back pain and keep the spine in shape. It comes with an attachment rod which can be adjusted for different kinds of exercises. It helps in several ways, like improved breathing and stress relief. The company claims that using IsoSpine for 10 minutes can have a major effect in a long span.

Fibonacci Coin - Fibonacci sequence is one of the most beautiful things in mathematics. It connects nature to a series and it is widely appreciated. Fibonacci coin is the representation of this series, there’s a measurable diagram embedded on the coin. It is a way to pay respects to people who’ve worked on it. It has a real life use as you can measure and explore the sequence by using that coin as the reference.

TALO - TALO brush is an automatic brushing assistance which brushes without any human effort. It finishes the job in 20 seconds and it is approved by dentists. It reduces the effort of stroking your brush on your tooth for the entire 2 mins. TALO brush targets every person who uses ordinary or electric brushes.

Eyeride HUD - Eyeride HUD creates a HUD display like a fighter jet helmet in any regular helmets. It uses the principle of TIR(total internal reflection) to display a screen on the inner part of the helmet's windshield. It can be used for navigation, answering phone calls, changing music track, etc. It can be connected to a smart phone and has voice controlled assistance. It is targeted on bike riders and it provides convenience and safety to bikers as they don’t need to look at their phone while driving.

Gravel Travel System - It is an entire system of backpacks to ease the burden of travelling and make it more comfortable. It has several subdivisions of packing which provides a wide range of functionality and utility. It is targeted towards almost every person who travels to different places.

ISOLATOR CUPS - These are ceramic cups designed to reduce the chances of getting burned by pouring hot beverage in them. It reduces thermal emission by using a unique spike design. It comes with a lid and a saucer to make it more convenient to use. It solves the problem of holding hot cups.

Simplelink - Simplelink is a two way magnetic cable which has a wide range of HDMI, SSD and other general ports connections. It has a transfer speed of 10GBps which makes transfering files easier and quicker. It is completely magnetic and it solves the problem of keeping and managing multiple. It has kevlar outer wires which makes it almost impossible to break unintentionally.

Social distancing badge - As the spread of COVID19 is a huge concern, social distancing becomes the utmost priority. WHO has officially said that we should at least maintain a distance of 1 meter from any person. This badge tells the distance from the nearest body by using laser based LiDAR technology to take precise measurements.

DeskSpace: lunar surface - DeskSpace creates remarkable models of lunar surface which are almost completely accurate as they are made from reading from NASA. It’s main purpose is to pay respect to the crew of Apollo 11 and celebrate and cherish the giant leap of mankind on 20th july 1969.

ShowerShroom - Showershroom is a method to reduce the risk of clogging of the drainage system. It uses a simple rubber blocker with several holes from which the waste materials can be removed.

AirBander - Airbander is a small yet innovative device used to holder paper planes and release them in an ideal way. It helps children to experience more ways to fly a plane without getting frustrated about not being able to launch them.

Beartooth rod - it is a unique fishing rod which folds in a 8 inch rod. It is used for tenkara fishing which involves nothing but a rod, a bait and a wire. Beartooth is made from carbon so it is highly durable, It can catch fish of upto 15 inches.

Copper linens - Copper linens are bed sheets created with egyptian cotton, earth’s softest cotton and pure copper alloy. It prevents bacterial growth in bed sheets as copper acts as an anion and destroys cell membranes of bacteria. It gives a fresh smell to the sheets every time and can actually benefit the skin due to copper’s anti aging and healing properties on human skin. Copper linens target almost every person.

Traqpod - traqpod is the new generation of skateboards it targets young people and makes it easy to learn skateboarding for beginners. It has thick wheels to ease the stress and help the board to turn better. It is flexible and has a unique design to attract new consumers. Traqpod solves the frustration of learning how to skateboard for beginners and helps amateur skateboarders to master the skill.
